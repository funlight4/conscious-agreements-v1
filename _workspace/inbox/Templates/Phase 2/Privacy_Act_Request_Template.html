PRIVACY ACT REQUEST

For All Government Records About You

5 U.S.C. ¬ß 552a

üîê What is a Privacy Act Request?

The Privacy Act gives you the right to:

  1. Access ALL records the government has about you
  2. Correct inaccurate records in government systems
  3. Find out who has accessed your records
  4. Discover surveillance or monitoring of your activities

This is broader than FOIA - it covers ALL records about YOU specifically

Privacy Act requests often reveal:
  • IRS records showing different income than reported
  • Secret surveillance or monitoring
  • Suspicious Activity Reports (SARs) filed by banks
  • Cross-agency information sharing
  • Government contracts or benefits you didn't know about

  1. Send to multiple agencies - Each maintains different records
  2. Keep certified mail receipts - Proof of delivery

  4. Review carefully - Look for surprises or inaccuracies
  5. Request corrections - If information is wrong
  6. Use in your defense - Government's own records are powerful evidence
