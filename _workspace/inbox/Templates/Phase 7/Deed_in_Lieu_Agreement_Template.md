## üí° TAX IMPLICATIONS EXPLAINED

### Cancellation of Debt Income (CODI):

**The Problem:**
- IRS treats forgiven debt as income
- Could owe taxes on difference
- Example: $400K debt - $300K value = $100K "income"

**Exceptions Available:**

1. **Qualified Principal Residence**
   - Primary home only
   - Up to $2 million forgiven
   - Must check current law

2. **Insolvency Exception**
   - Debts exceed assets
   - Complete Form 982
   - No tax on forgiven amount

3. **Bankruptcy Exception**
   - Debt discharged in bankruptcy
   - No tax consequences
   - Consider before deed in lieu

**Action Required:**
- Consult tax professional
- File Form 982 if applicable
- Keep all documentation
