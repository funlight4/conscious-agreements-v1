## üéØ SETTLEMENT STRATEGIES

### Leverage Points:
- Evidence of lack of consideration
- Costs of continued litigation
- Publicity risks for bank
- Regulatory complaints filed
- Class action potential
- Criminal referrals made

### Common Settlement Terms:
- **Principal Reduction:** 40-80% typical
- **Payment Plans:** 0-36 months
- **Lien Release:** Immediate upon payment
- **Credit Reporting:** "Paid as Agreed" best
- **Tax Forgiveness:** 1099-C waiver valuable
- **Confidentiality:** Bank usually wants this

### Red Flags to Avoid:
- Admission of guilt clauses
- Unlimited indemnification
- Waiver of statutory rights
- No time limits for performance
- Vague or incomplete terms
- Missing credit reporting provisions
