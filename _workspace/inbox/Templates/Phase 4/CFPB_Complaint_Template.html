HOW TO SUBMIT

Primary Method - Online (Recommended):

  1. Go to: www.consumerfinance.gov/complaint
  2. Select "Mortgage" as product
  3. Select appropriate sub-products and issues
  4. Enter company information
  5. Describe your complaint
  6. Upload supporting documents
  7. Submit and get complaint number

Alternative Methods:

  • Phone: (855) 411-CFPB (2372)
  • Mail: Consumer Financial Protection Bureau, P.O. Box 27170, Washington, DC 20038
  • Fax: (855) 237-2392

WHAT HAPPENS NEXT

Timeline:

  • Immediately: You receive complaint number
  • Within 15 days: Company must respond
  • Within 60 days: Most complaints closed
  • Ongoing: You can check status online

Possible Outcomes:

  • Company provides relief requested
  • CFPB investigation opened
  • Enforcement action initiated
  • Pattern identified for supervision
  • Referral to other agencies

üí° Tips for Effective CFPB Complaints

  1. Be specific - Include dates, amounts, names
  2. Attach evidence - Documents strengthen complaint
  3. Show pattern - Not just your issue
  4. Calculate damages - Be comprehensive
  5. Reference laws - Shows serious violations
  6. Update regularly - Add new violations
  7. Track response - Hold company accountable
  8. Share publicly - Helps other consumers
  9. Follow up - Don't let it drop
  10. Escalate if needed - Contact CFPB directly
