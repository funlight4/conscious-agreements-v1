Terminology

Legal terms often mean something different than their common usage. Always verify definitions before assuming you understand what a document says.

Most Critical Terms

  • Consideration — Without it, no valid contract exists
  • Person — May not mean what you think
  • Citizen / Resident — Status with implications
  • Understand — "Standing under" authority
  • Must / Includes — Often mean the opposite
  • Negotiable Instrument — What your signature creates
Primary Source
Black's Law Dictionary, 4th Edition

The authoritative reference for legal terminology. The 4th Edition is preferred for its comprehensive definitions and historical context.
