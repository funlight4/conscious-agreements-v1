Understanding the Tiers

Tier 1: Foundation

These are the core concepts that everything else builds on. You don't need to master all of them immediately, but understanding "What is Agreement?" and "Conscious vs Unconscious" gives you the lens to see everything else clearly. The Money Creation section is especially critical for mortgage challenges.

Tier 2: Domain Knowledge

These are deep dives into specific areas of law and agreement. Commerce covers the mechanics of contracts, negotiable instruments, and the UCC. Identity explores legal personhood and status. You don't need all domains ‚Äî pick the one relevant to your situation.

Tier 3: Practical Pathways

These are step-by-step guides for specific situations. They assume you have the foundation and relevant domain knowledge. If you jump straight here without the background, you'll know what to do but not why ‚Äî which makes it harder to adapt when things don't go as expected.

Tier 4: Templates & Actions

These are the actual documents you'll use. They're organized into 7 phases that build on each other. Using templates without understanding the pathway is like using a map without knowing where you are ‚Äî technically possible, but risky.
