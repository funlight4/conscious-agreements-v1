The Universal Approach

For any situation, the approach follows the same pattern:

  1. Examine the claimed agreement ‚Äî What are they claiming? On what basis?
  2. Identify the parties ‚Äî Who is claiming, and who are they claiming against?
  3. Check consideration ‚Äî What did each party give? Is it genuine?
  4. Verify standing ‚Äî Does the claimant have the right to enforce?
  5. Respond strategically ‚Äî Without admitting, without waiving rights
  6. Document everything ‚Äî Create records of all communications
