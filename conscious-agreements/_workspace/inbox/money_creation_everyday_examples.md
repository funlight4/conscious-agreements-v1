## Morning Coffee: Two Ways to Pay, Two Different Worlds

### 7:15 AM - Starbucks Run

**Scenario A: You pay with a DEBIT card**
- You tap your card for a $6 latte
- $6 moves from your account to Starbucks
- This $6 already existed (created from someone's old promise)
- Total money in the world: Unchanged
- You just moved existing promises around

**Scenario B: You pay with a CREDIT card**
- You tap your card for a $6 latte
- You just created a NEW promise to pay $6
- Starbucks gets $6 immediately (minus fees)
- Total money in the world: Increased by $6
- **You literally just created money while buying coffee**

*Multiply this by millions of coffee purchases daily, and you see how the money supply grows every morning.*
