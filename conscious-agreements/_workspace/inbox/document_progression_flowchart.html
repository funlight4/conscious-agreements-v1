PHASE 1: Information Gathering
Weeks 1-4
REQUEST Day 1
Qualified Written Request (QWR)
RESPA - 30 day response required
Purpose: Obtain complete loan documentation
Send to: Loan servicer
Key items: Payment history, chain of title, securitization docs
REQUEST Day 1-3
Debt Validation Letter
FDCPA - If debt collector involved
Purpose: Force proof of valid debt
Timeline: Within 30 days of first contact
Effect: Stops collection during validation
REQUEST Week 1
FOIA Requests Bundle
Fed, OCC, FDIC, SEC, CFPB
Targets: Federal Reserve, OCC, FDIC, SEC
Seeking: Discount window activity, complaints, audits
Timeline: 20 business days typically
PHASE 2: Establishing Position
Weeks 4-8
AFFIDAVIT Week 4
Affidavit of Truth
Sworn statement - Notarized
Contains: Facts about money creation
States: Promissory note IS payment
Record at: County recorder
NOTICE Week 5
Notice of Lack of Consideration
To CEO & Board - 30 day cure
Demands: Proof of consideration
Or: Mark account paid in full
Warning: Criminal violations listed
NOTICE Week 6
Notice of Criminal Violations
Specific USC violations cited
Lists: 18 USC 1025, 1584, 894, 1341
Effect: Puts bank on legal notice
Creates: Liability for continued acts
PHASE 3: Default & Escalation
Weeks 8-12
NOTICE Day 31
Notice of Default
Bank failed to prove consideration
Effect: Documents admission of fraud
Creates: Estoppel against bank
Next: Criminal complaints
COMPLAINT Week 9
Criminal Complaints Bundle
FBI, DOJ, US Attorney, State AG
FBI: Banking fraud, mail fraud
DOJ: Slavery, peonage, rights violations
US Attorney: Local federal prosecution
State AG: State law violations
COMPLAINT Week 10
Regulatory Complaints
CFPB, OCC, Federal Reserve
CFPB: Consumer protection
OCC: National bank violations
Effect: Regulatory investigation pressure
