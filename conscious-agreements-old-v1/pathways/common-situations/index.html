General Approach

For any situation, the approach is similar:

  1. Examine the claimed agreement — What are they claiming? See Examining Any Agreement
  2. Identify the parties — Who is claiming, and who are they claiming against?
  3. Check consideration — What did each party give? Is it genuine?
  4. Verify standing — Does the claimant have the right to enforce?
  5. Respond appropriately — Without admitting, without waiving rights. See Responding to Claims
  6. Document everything — Create records of all communications
