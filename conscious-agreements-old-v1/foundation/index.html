Foundation

The core concepts that make everything else make sense. Read these first — they establish the lens through which to view all agreements.

Before diving into specific legal domains or practical pathways, you need the fundamental reframe. These concepts aren't just theory — they're the foundation for every practical step that follows.

The Core Insight

Most of what we call "law" functions as agreement. And most people are in agreements they never consciously entered. Understanding this changes everything about how you navigate.

Foundation Topics

What Is an Agreement?

The fundamental nature of agreements — what makes something an agreement, the elements required, and how they differ from commands or decrees.
Conscious vs. Unconscious

The critical distinction. How agreements you knowingly enter differ from those you stumbled into — and why this matters for your options.
How You Energize Agreements

Agreements don't persist on their own — they require your participation. Understanding how you "energize" them reveals how you might stop.
Covert Contracts

Hidden agreements you didn't know you made. How they form, how to recognize them, and what to do once you see them.
Healthy Agreements

What a good agreement looks like. Mutual benefit, clear terms, genuine consent, and valid consideration. The standard to measure against.
Money Creation

How money is actually created through your signature. Why this matters for understanding consideration and "debt" agreements.
