## The Perspective Framework

### The 12 Universal Perspectives

**1. First Person (I/Me)**
- Personal experience
- Subjective truth
- Internal feelings
- Individual needs
Example: "I feel hurt by this"

**2. Second Person (You)**
- Other's experience
- Their truth
- Their feelings
- Their needs
Example: "You must feel frustrated"

**3. Third Person (He/She/They)**
- Observer view
- Objective facts
- Neutral assessment
- System patterns
Example: "They're both struggling"

**4. Fourth Person (We)**
- Collective experience
- Shared reality
- Group needs
- Community view
Example: "We're all learning here"

**5. Temporal Past**
- Historical view
- What led here
- Pattern recognition
- Karma/consequences
Example: "This stems from childhood"

**6. Temporal Future**
- Potential outcomes
- Long-term effects
- Vision perspective
- Evolution trajectory
Example: "In 10 years this won't matter"

**7. Higher Self**
- Soul perspective
- Evolution purpose
- Learning available
- Highest good
Example: "This is perfect for growth"

**8. Inner Child**
- Innocent view
- Simple truth
- Basic needs
- Pure feeling
Example: "This isn't fun"

**9. Shadow**
- Hidden aspects
- Denied truths
- Projections
- Unconscious drivers
Example: "What I hate in them is in me"

**10. Divine/God**
- Ultimate perspective
- Perfect orchestration
- Love underlying all
- Paradise vision
Example: "All is unfolding perfectly"

**11. Practical/Pragmatic**
- What works
- Resource reality
- Action needed
- Immediate steps
Example: "Let's focus on what we can control"

**12. Energetic/Vibrational**
- Frequency view
- Energy dynamics
- Resonance patterns
- Field effects
Example: "The energy here needs clearing"
